Beyond Bitesize

Submission for the D&AD Awards for 2022. The brief was to create a learning experience for the BBC that expands upon their Bitesize platform - one that is fun, personalised, engaging & inclusive to children of all ages. I designed the app with sections for holistic learning of all aspects, including regular academic subjects and life skills. There are inclusive, customisable characters, parent feedback and positive reinforcement.
